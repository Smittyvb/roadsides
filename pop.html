Planes on Pedestals


A collection of photos of airforce and civilian aircraft found on pedestals across Canada. If you have photos that you would like to share or are aware of where I may be able to obtain more to add to the site or photos of better quality, please send me an email. Please check my wanted photos of planes list. Of course, I will give credit where due.

NOTE: Many of the specific details and locations of the "Planes on Pedestals" were obtained from Hal Skaarup's book titled "Canadian Warbirds Survivors 2000 (A handbook on where to find them)" which can be obtained through Amazon.com and iUniverse.com. An electronic version of the book was graciously provided by the author.
