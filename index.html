Planes on Pedestals



Blog

Across Canada, there are hundreds of large and interesting roadside attractions. For almost 20 years, this site has been dedicated to cataloguing our nation’s large roadside attractions.

We have also included a separate collection that features mainly military and civilian aircraft that are on pedestals: planes on sticks.

Send us your photos of any roadside attractions that we have not yet listed. Send us better photos of roadside attractions that we already have. Send us any better info you may have about any of the roadside attractions -- including their history and their exact location (GPS coordinates, if you have them). And let us know if any the roadside attractions are gone and need to be archived.

Visit the archive to see photos of roadside attractions that no longer exist.

Want to keep track of what we’re up to, and what’s new on the site? Check out our blog, the What’s New page, our Facebook page, or follow us on Twitter.
