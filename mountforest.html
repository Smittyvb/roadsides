Fruit Basket
Mount Forest, Ontario

410 Sligo Rd W., Mount Forest, Ontario, at Wellington Wood Products. The basket was installed at Wellington Wood Products in September of 1986. The basket was designed and built by Mount Forest resident Nelson Moore, who was a long-time friend of the factory owner.
